About ICAM Technologies Corporation

ICAM Technologies Corporation specializes in the development and implementation of advanced NC post-processing and integrated machine tool simulation solutions for manufacturers in major industries around the world. ICAM customers benefit from a dramatic improvement to CNC machine optimization, NC programmer productivity and manufacturing process efficiency. ICAM's unique technology and services provide its customers and industry partners with the competitive advantages that their business operations and customers demand.

Original Software Development Partnership With Dassault Systèmes

Innovation - ICAM's OSD (Original Software Development) partnership with Dassault Systèmes, provides the CATIA V5 NC Manufacturing user with unparalleled ease of use and integrated access to the industry's most advanced NC post-processing technology.

Integration - The CAM-POST CATIA V5 direct product interface is aimed at facilitating post-processor deployment and management in a streamlined, user-friendly environment. The result is clear, never before achieved levels of NC programmer productivity and manufacturing process efficiency.

Optimization - Through ICAM's advanced technology and best practices, customers are leveraging the benefits offered by CATIA V5 NC and seamlessly transitioning that value into proven CNC machine tool optimization for improved part quality and production output.

Evolution - Through on-going OSD development initiatives with Dassault Systèmes, ICAM's cutting edge technology will continue to set new standards for product collaboration and advance the competitive advantage that our customer and industry partners heavily depend upon.
