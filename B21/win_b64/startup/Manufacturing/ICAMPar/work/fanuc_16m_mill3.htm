fanuc_16m_mill3 demonstration post-processor

The fanuc_16m_mill3 post-processor produces output for a generic 3-axis mill with an Fanuc Ltd. Model 16 Series M controller.

This post-processor outputs MM units only.

This is a demonstration post-processor - output is limited to 300 blocks.
