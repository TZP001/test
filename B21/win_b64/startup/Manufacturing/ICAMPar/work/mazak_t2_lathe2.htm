mazak_t2_lathe2 demonstration post-processor

The mazak_t2_lathe2 post-processor produces output for a generic 2-axis lathe with a Yamazaki Mazak Corporation Mazatrol CAM T-2 controller.

The cross-slide control is output as a radius value.

This post-processor outputs INCH units only.

This is a demonstration post-processor - output is limited to 300 blocks.
