mits_600m_mill3 demonstration post-processor

The mits_600m_mill3 post-processor produces output for a generic 3-axis mill with a Mitsubishi Electric Co. Meldas 600 Series M controller.

This post-processor outputs INCH units only.

This is a demonstration post-processor - output is limited to 300 blocks.
