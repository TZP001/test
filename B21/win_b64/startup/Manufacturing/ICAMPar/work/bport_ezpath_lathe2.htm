bport_ezpath_lathe2 demonstration post-processor

The bport_ezpath_lathe2 post-processor produces output for a generic 2-axis lathe with a Bridgeport Machines EZ-Path controller.

The cross-slide control is output as a diameter value.

This post-processor outputs either INCH or MM units, depending on the units of the CL file.

This is a demonstration post-processor - output is limited to 300 blocks.
